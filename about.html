About Movie Finding Made Easy

Discover the story behind our mission to make movie discovery effortless.

Our Mission

Movie Finding Made Easy is your easy and fast way to discover, explore, and enjoy your favorite movies. We aim to make movie searching simple and fun by offering a clean, responsive interface where users can find information about movies, genres, ratings, and links to external resources like IMDb or YouTube trailers.

Technologies Used

  • HTML5 for structure and semantic layout
  • CSS3 for responsive and modern design
  • JavaScript for interactivity (search, filters, modals)
  • PHP for backend processing and dynamic content

Design Philosophy

This site uses a dark, cinematic theme inspired by the atmosphere of movie theatres — combining clarity, contrast, and accessibility. Typography follows a clear hierarchy using semantic HTML tags to meet web accessibility standards.

Developer

Developed as part of the Information Technology Diploma (Web Management 3.2) at VUT. This website was created as part of the Web Development 3.2 Final Project and demonstrates practical skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.
